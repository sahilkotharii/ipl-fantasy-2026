How to Play
🏏 Complete Guide
01 🙋
Create Your Account
Sign up with your name, a unique team name, and a 4-digit PIN. Your account links to the shared Google Sheet — no email needed.
02 🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Wait for the Auction
Once all managers join, the Admin runs the player auction. Players are sold to the highest bidder and your squad is locked after the draft.
03 👑
Pick Captain & Vice-Captain
From your squad, choose a Captain (2× points) and a Vice-Captain (1.5× points). Update them any time from the My Squad page.
04 📊
Earn Points
After each IPL match, the Admin updates player stats. Your players score fantasy points based on runs, wickets, catches, and more.
05 🏆
Climb the Leaderboard
Track your rank live on the Dashboard. The fantasy manager with the most total points at the end of IPL 2026 wins the prize pool!
06 💡
Tips to Win
Pick all-rounders for maximum points. Rotate C/VC based on match-ups. Watch the Players page to track form and catch top scorers.
