📖 How to Play

1
Sign up & join the league
Create an account with your name and a team name. Your admin will share the app link — everyone plays in the same private league.
2
Draft your players at auction
Go to Squad → tap any player → enter your bid price to buy them. Once a player is bought they're locked to your team. Build your squad before the tournament starts.
3
Pick a Captain & Vice Captain
In your Squad page, set one player as Captain and one as Vice Captain. Your Captain earns 2× points and your VC earns 1.5× points for every match.
4
Enter match stats after each game
After a match, go to Stats and tap the ＋ button next to each of your players. Enter runs, wickets, catches etc. and hit Add Stats — points are calculated automatically.
5
Track the leaderboard
Check Home for the live leaderboard, Orange Cap (top run scorer), Purple Cap (top wicket taker), and other tournament leaders.
6
Made a mistake? Use Edit mode
Tap the ✏️ button next to a player in Stats to open Edit mode. It pre-fills the current stats — correct any value and hit Replace. Points recalculate from scratch automatically.
💡 Tip: The points each stat is worth is shown in the Points Table above. Your admin can change these anytime from the Admin scoring rules section.
